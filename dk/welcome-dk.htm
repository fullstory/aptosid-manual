Velkommen til den sidux-manual

Be prepared!

sidux will challenge your belief system compared with every other release you have previously tried or used. sidux is not only based on Debian and Debian 'sid' GNU/Linux, sidux holds fast to the core values and social contract of Debian.

You no longer need to wait for a new release to always have the latest of anything, including kernels. Once you have installed sidux, all it needs is a dist-upgrade, which is a system-wide software update from debian and sidux.

This means that re-installing yet another release every 6-12 months on your PC is unnecessary with sidux, as the weekly or monthly dist-upgrade brings everything up-to-date, except the kernel; which may be upgraded separately as required.

As it is, an install with sidux usually does not take longer than 20 minutes.

sidux offers 4 up-to-date live-cd entry point release variations, these are:
32 bit lite,
32 bit full,
64 bit lite,
64 bit full

With the 'way of sidux', you will be always up to date and have the very best that sidux together with Debian 'sid' can offer.

Welcome to sidux!

Anvendelse af manualen

Manualen er inddelt i sektioner der har samme overordnede tema. For eksempel findes alt information der har med partitionering at gøre under "partitionering af harddiske", og emner som WIFI findes under "Internet og Netværk".

Der er nogle emner der ikke kan grupperes eller kræver at stå alene. Som med alt andet ny dokumentation vil der være fejl/misforståelser/tastefejl, men vi håber ikke at der er nogle virkelige fejl i blandt (tastefejl kan vi leve med i starten).

Efterhånden som sidux udvikler sig vil der blive tilføjet mere dokumentation, og vi fra sidux-team'et er sikre på at det vil blive en værdifuld ressource for alle brugere.

Vi takker dig for at være en del af sidux.
