Cheatcodes

Ako su moguće vrijednosti navedeni pod "Vrijednost", morate ih dodati uz "cheatcode" sa =. Na primjer, ako je 1280x1024 povoljena vrijednost za screen cheatcode, onda morate upisati screen=1280x1024 u grub boot prompt, or for language, lang=pt.

Reference to an exhaustive list for kernel boot codes

sidux specific cheatcodes (Live-cd only)

Cheatcode Vrijednost Opis
blacklist modul-ime da deaktivirate module prije udev
desktop kde izaberite desktop
fluxbox
fromiso Molimo da se odnosite na bootanje sa "fromiso"
hostname mojehostime hostime, promjeni hostime live-cd sistema
lang be, bg, cz, da, de, de_CH, el, en, en_AU, en_GB, en_IE, es, fr, fr_BE, ga, hr, hu, it, ja, nl, nl_BE, pl, pt (pt_BR), pt_PT, ro, ru, zh Sets your language. It will also set locale, keyboard layout (console and X), timezone and a debian mirror.
When the extended form lang=ll_cc or lang=ll-cc is given, ll will be used for language selection and cc for keyboard, mirror and timezone selection (e.g. lang=fr-be). The defaults for English are en_US with UTC as the timezone and for German, de with Europe/Berlin as the timezone. Example: lang=pt_PT tz=Pacific/Auckland

toram kopiraj CD u RAM i startuj sa nje
tz tz=Europe/Dublin Sets your timezone. If your bios/hardware clock is set to UTC, use utc=yes. List of all timezones supported.
X related cheatcodes

Either the xrandr or xmodule cheatcode should also be used if using other X related cheatcodes with a radeon, intel or mga graphics card.

Cheatcode Vrijednost Opis
dpi auto or XX Set desired Dots Per Inch for your display. You can work out your screen's dpi from the width in pixels divided by the diagonal size in inches and multiplying this by 1.25 for a 4:3 screen, 1.18 for a 16:10 screen or 1.147 for a 16:9 screen. For a 24" 1920x1080 screen it would be 1.147*1920/24 giving dpi=92 or a 15" 1600x1200 screen would be 1.25*1600/15 giving dpi=133.
hsync 80 (zB) horizontalna frekvencija osvježavanja monitora (kHz)
noml Onemogući X.org konfiguraciji da sadrži Modelinije i izaziva audodetekciju točnog modusa
noxrandr deaktiviraj korištaj RandR 1.2 ekstenzije novih X.org pogonitelja i koristi legacy monitor probing tehnike
screen 800x600 postavi monitor rezoluciju, 1024x768, 1600x1200 itd
vsync 60 vertikalna brzina osvježavanja monitora (Hz)
xdepth vrijednosti: 8 15 16 24 Standardna dubina boja koja je korištena od X.org (poneki pogonitelji ne podržavaju 1 i 4)
keytable (na primjer, us, de, gb) Keyboard Layout korišten od X.org
xkbmodel (eg) pc105 Keyboard model korišten od X.org
xkboptions (e.g.) grp:alt_shift_toggle Keyboard variant to be used by X.org
xkbvariant (e.g.) nodeadkeys, to set a keyboard variant
xmode (eg 800x600) Postavi rezoluciju monitora, 1024x768, 1600x1200 itd
xmodule or xdriver ati, fbdev, i810, mga, nv, radeon, savage, vesa koristi dodan X-modul
xrandr Prisili X.org da bude konfiguriran da koristi RandR 1.2 ekstenzije novih X.org pogonitelja
xrate XX Prisili povlaštenu ratu osvježavanja od RandR 1.2 podržanu od X.org pogonitelja, mora se koristiti zajedno sa xmode cheatcode Sveobuhvatno dokumentacija je ovdje
xhrefresh 80 horizontalna brzina osvježavanja monitora (kHz)
xvrefresh 60 vertikalna brzina osvježavanja monitora
