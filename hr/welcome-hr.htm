Dobro došli kod sidux™ Operating System Priručnika

sidux is an operating system based on the Linux kernel and the GNU project, with applications/programs from the Debian unstable branch, called "Sid", and holds fast to the core values and social contract of Debian.

You no longer need to wait for a new release to always have the latest of anything, including kernels. Once you have installed sidux, followed by all your favourite programs/applications, all it needs is a dist-upgrade, which is a system-wide software update from debian and specialist packages from sidux.

This means that re-installing yet another release every 6+ months on your PC is unnecessary with sidux, as the daily, weekly or monthly dist-upgrade brings everything up-to-date.

It is worth noting, as sidux uses Debian's unstable branch and due to the very nature of 'Sid', you will need to be prepared to use the Terminal/cli

With the 'way of sidux', you will be always up to date and have the very best that sidux together with Debian 'Sid' can offer.

Kako koristiti ovaj priručnik

The sidux Operating System Manual is a reference for initial learning and refreshing your knowledge of the sidux Operating System, not only with the basics, as it also encompasses many complex topics and will help you as the sidux system administrator.

For help with a specific program or an application (called a package) that came preinstalled or that you have installed yourself, look at the website of the package help forums, FAQs and online manuals and/or a help menu within that package, to help guide you for any given program/application.

Most good programs/applications also have a help guide via man pages in the terminal. Also check to see if documentation is in /usr/share/doc/<package>.

Please, if you do nothing else, take a moment to read the Quick Start Guide

Ovaj priručnik je podjelen u zajedničke sekcije:, na primjer, teme o particijama će se naći pod Particijoniraj vaš Hard Disk, a teme o Internet i WIFI su grupirane pod Internet i umrežavanje.

Imamo teme koje se ne mogu grupirati i potrebno je da imaju stand-alone-status. S svakom novom dokumentacijom bit ce greški i pogreška pri upisu, nadamo se da nema prave greške, sa pogreškama pri upisu možemo zivjeti.

Čim posao raste, više documentacije će mo sastaviti, i mi od sidux team-a vjerujemo da će vam to biti dobar resurs znanja o sidux-u.

Tiskanje Starnica od Priručnika

Za Tiskanje Stranicasa Printerom na uspravni format nije dozvalja tiskanje od predugog Coda na printerski papir jer printer nema automatiziran prijelom retka .

Ali ovo nije problem koji ćete imati na Internet stranici, na matici medijskog sita,koji je manje kritičan za svaku linux distribuciju da se kopira code i stavi u terminal možda duži 120 slova, tako je u glavnom širi od tipične A4 starnice sa jednom običnom veličinom od 12 pt.

Prema tome namjestite vaš printer za takvo tiskanje landscape,.
